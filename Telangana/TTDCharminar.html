Things to do near Charminar






Hyderabad is a historical city which has brought along cuisines, monuments, culture, traditions and many more things from the past. Here are some of the exclusive things you could do in the city near Charminar:




×

Travel

Charminar is located in the heart of the city and all the renowned places are in the vicinity of the monument. So, it is highly suggested that you should visit all of them like Golconda Fort, Mecca Masjid, etc.

.




×

Shop

As the monument is settled in the middle of the Laad Bazaar, you may like to shop for some souvenirs for your friends and families. Pearls, Lacquer Bangles, Bidri artifacts, soorma, and many more things are there in the market which you can stuff in your bags to take back home.

.




×

Eat

Hyderabad has a legacy of delicacies from the kitchen of Mughals. You would love to eat various splendid recipes prepared with authentic techniques. Hyderabadi Biryani is one of the most famous cuisines in the city.
