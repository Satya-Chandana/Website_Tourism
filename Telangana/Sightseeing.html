SightSeeing

Make a visit to Golconda Fort, witness the Qutb Shahi Tombs, indulge in the royalty of Chowmahalla Palace, wander around Charminar – the landmark of Hyderabad, make a roundabout of Hussain Sagar Lake, exploring tank bund, sanjeevaiah park, necklace road, Jalavihar, NTR Gardens, Lumbini Park, take a boat ride in the waters of Hussain Sagar Lake with a roundabout of lord Buddha in the middle of the lake, explore the rendezvous of Durgam Cheruvu – the secret lake, immerse in the enthralling water parks of the city, visit Birla temple, rediscover the antiques of Salarjung Museum, engage with the animal world of the Nehru Zoological Park, walk-in to the KBR National Park and KVBR Botanical Garden, move out to the city outskirts and visit Ramoji Film City for a fun – filled day, visit Sanghi temple, engage with the flora and fauna of the national parks of Mahaveer Harina Vanasthali and Mrugavani, make a wish visiting lord Balaji at Chilukuru, indulge in adventure activities through any of the rock climbing clubs in and around Hyderabad like Rock Climbing School at Bhongir Fort that engages you with an exciting and thrilling experience with boulders and natural settings offering spectacular activities. Connect to other parts of Telangana. Embrace the heritage monuments, divine temples, cultural fragrances, lush green forests, rivers, wildlife, waterfalls, lakes and the rural life of Telangana.
