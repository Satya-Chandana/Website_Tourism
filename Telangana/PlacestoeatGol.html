Places to Eat




Little Italy

Despite its vegetarian only tag, Little Italy remains the favourite for most Hyderabadis, when it comes to Italian fine-dining. The exhaustive menu crust pizzas, act as great appetite enhancers. For sweet endings, a tiramisu sponge cake soaked in rum and coffee, topped with vanilla creme custard and sprinkled with chocolate powder is a must!

Tatva

Tatva is a fresh amalgamation of traditional diversity and contemporary elements that brings all your senses to life. The cafe emanates an aura of young vibes. If you are looking for unique mocktails with a blend of different ingredients, an exclusive selection of food and interiors that radiate vibrance then this is the place to be.

China Bistro

This is the Hyderabad outlet of the Mumbai based Food link restaurant group, which has four branches of China Bistro in Mumbai. There are 2 huge statues of terracotta warriors guarding the entrance of the restaurant. Inside there is a war horse statue in the main hall.The food served is essentially Chinese with some Pan-Asian dishes thrown in.
