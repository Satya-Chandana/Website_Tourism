Best Time to Visit Meenakshi Temple

The Meenakshi Thirukalyanam festival commemorates Meenakshi Amman's sacred marriage to Lord Shiva. This grand festival takes place in Madurai, Tamil Nadu's cultural capital, and is one of the city's most important activities. According to legend, Lord Shiva once came to Madurai to tie the nuptial knots with Meenakshi, and this practise has been carried on ever since. This annual event is held in Madurai's popular "Meenakshi Amman Temple," and devotees from all over the world attend to witness this magnificent event. Meenakshi Thirukalyanam is held on the 10th day of the renowned Chithirai festival.
